Cross-Laminated Timber · Shear Analogy & Gamma Method

Analytical Method

Shear Analogy: Supports 3 – 9 layers. Layup must be symmetric (top-to-bottom mirror). Calculates both EIeff and GAeff.

Gamma (γ) Method: Supports 3 or 5 layers only. Based on Eurocode 5 Annex B. Requires a span length to compute the γ factor. Calculates EIeff only.
